NIKE Running
Shoes.

To keep you comfortable for long adventures on the trail, designers used Nike’s React foam for a soft and smooth underfoot feel and added a Zoom Air unit under the forefoot for better responsiveness and feedback on uneven terrain. Designers carved the midsole into a rocker shape to help smooth out transitions and add a bit of propulsion. Plus, they gave the Infinity Run 3 extra width to make it more stable, meaning it works well for people who overpronate and neutral runners alike. The Flyknit upper is breathable and provides extra support and security with a locked-down fit throughout the midfoot.
